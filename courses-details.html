A typical week at Madesoft Academy

  • Live Workshops: Physical Workshop to consolidate the learning will be conducted in Batches. Due to widespread of our student a separate time table for Workshops and locations shall be communicated by Instructor.
  • Virtual Classes: By illustrating concepts and the real-time use-case demonstration Interact, mentors can lead you through live interactive lessons to develop a sound understanding of concepts on a weekly basis.
  • Structured Intervention: Apply the learnings from Online Classrooms by designing tasks and projects of your own. This will be supported by formal intervention sessions with our mentors which include task / assignment explainer and support.
  • Individual Learning On LMS: Learn predefined challenging content by yourself. This part will be carefully tailored to your personal progress. Also the structured Intervention where you can reach out to your mentor in the case of any challenges.
  • Personal Exercise/Assignments: Solve predefined challenging Exercise/Assignment by yourself. Also the structured Intervention where you can reach out to your mentor in the case of any challenges.
  • Personal Lab Work: Solve predefined challenging Lab work by yourself. Also the structured Intervention where you can reach out to your mentor in the case of any challenges.
