Industrial Automation & PLCs Programming

Finance

Industrial Automation & PLCs Programming.

Description:

The goal of this 6 Months full-time program is to turn you into a fully operational Full Stack Industrial Automation & PLCs Programmer. Graduates of this course typically find employment as Automation or PLCs Engineer within a very short time frame. The lessons taught cover a diverse set of technologies which make for the perfect springboard onto the automation scene.

Curriculum:

Through an advanced curriculum and project-based structure, students learn today’s cutting-edge Automation technologies. Our curriculum provides an indept learning to Industrial Automation including digital electronic, programming of PLCs, Human Machine Interface (HMI) & SCADA systems.

Certification:

Students will receive a verifiable and shareable certificate from the Academy upon completion of the course. Employers will be able to validate this certificate by using the student's unique identification number.

Course Instructor:

3 Months Intensive Lessons

This Program Consist of Online Virtual Classes, modularised lessons managed by Learning Management System with assignments, downloadable study materials and video lectures to fully engage the student on weekly basis. Student will be involved in lab work after each key concept is taught, the lab work shall be in the form of practicals with guide from instructors, each assignment and lab work shall be used as part of performance and assessment grading.

3 Months Project Based Learning.

Student will gain knowledge and skills by working for 3 months to investigate and respond with a solution to an authentic industry related problem. Student shall engage with project management tools to enable them gain understanding of tasks, timelines, schedules with a project Manager assigned. The learning will mirror what professionals do every day and prepares the students to accept and meet challenges in the real world.

A typical week at Madesoft Academy

  • Live Workshops: Physical Workshop to consolidate the learning will be conducted in Batches. Due to widespread of our student a separate time table for Workshops and locations shall be communicated by Instructor.
  • Virtual Classes: By illustrating concepts and the real-time use-case demonstration Interact, mentors can lead you through live interactive lessons to develop a sound understanding of concepts on a weekly basis.
  • Structured Intervention: Apply the learnings from Online Classrooms by designing tasks and projects of your own. This will be supported by formal intervention sessions with our mentors which include task / assignment explainer and support.
  • Individual Learning On LMS: Learn predefined challenging content by yourself. This part will be carefully tailored to your personal progress. Also the structured Intervention where you can reach out to your mentor in the case of any challenges.
  • Personal Exercise/Assignments: Solve predefined challenging Exercise/Assignment by yourself. Also the structured Intervention where you can reach out to your mentor in the case of any challenges.
  • Personal Lab Work: Solve predefined challenging Lab work by yourself. Also the structured Intervention where you can reach out to your mentor in the case of any challenges.
